Permission to Use Material from ICRP Publication 133
On behalf of the International Commission on Radiological Protection (ICRP), I am pleased to formally grant permission for MHI NS Engineering Co., Ltd. to use the following material from ICRP Publication 133:
Specific Absorbed Fractions (SAFs) and source/target region data
